Real Time Tracking & Branded Customer Experience

ProShip keeps you in the loop with real-time tracking, thanks to our native and deep integrations with courier partners. Get instant status updates directly on ProShip, ensuring you're always up to date with your shipments.

These updates are seamlessly passed on to your customer-facing platforms via webhooks and API integration, so your customers are never left in the dark. Plus, take advantage of ProShip's free branded, white-labeled tracking page, giving your customers a smooth, consistent experience under your brand. With ProShip, real-time tracking isn't just smart—it's effortless.

Returns Management Suite

Managing returns is a breeze with ProShip's white-labeled suite. You'll have access to multiple courier partners with deep expertise in reverse pickups, ensuring a smooth process from start to finish.

Our dynamic allocation engine selects the most optimal courier partner for each reverse pickup, while return reason-based QC rules allow you to customize quality checks according to the specifics of each return. ProShip seamlessly shares all necessary information—like product images, sizes, and tags—with the courier partner, so they can perform comprehensive checks right at the customer's doorstep.

If the product passes inspection, it's picked up; if not, the customer is promptly informed of the reasons for rejection. Throughout the process, real-time notifications keep your customers in the loop, enhancing their trust and satisfaction with your service.

Real Time Notifications

With ProShip's Real-Time Notifications, keep your customers informed every step of the way. Our system sends instant updates via SMS and WhatsApp, ensuring they always know the status of their shipment.

These notifications are not just real-time; they're fully customizable and branded to match your business, offering a seamless and professional experience that reinforces your brand identity.

Whether it's a delivery update, a return confirmation, or any other key event, your customers receive timely, relevant information that enhances their confidence and satisfaction with your service.

NDR & NPR Management

ProShip's Delivery and Pickup Failure Handling is designed to keep things on track, even when issues arise. Our NDR (Non-Delivery Report) and NPR (Non-Pickup Report) systems operate on multiple levels to ensure smooth resolution.

1. Tele Calling: We offer customized, brand-specific scripted tele-calling in regional languages, ensuring that your customers receive a personal touch when resolving issues.

2. WhatsApp: Additionally, our WhatsApp automated flow allows customers to provide further instructions effortlessly, whether it's reattempting delivery or pickup, updating an address, or even canceling the shipments.

3. Rules: For a truly hands-off approach, leverage ProShip's rule-based engine to automatically handle NDR and NPR scenarios based on complex, pre-defined rules. With these robust options, you can ensure every delivery or pickup failure is managed effectively, minimizing disruptions and maintaining customer satisfaction.

Performance Management & Business Intelligence

ProShip's Control Tower is your command center for identifying shipments at risk, powered by advanced algorithms that monitor every stage—pickup, handover, delivery, and RTO delivery.

Say goodbye to sifting through Excel sheets to find problematic shipments. Our Control Tower goes beyond simply flagging SLA breaches; it proactively identifies risks like multiple delivery attempts before RTO, shipments with no movement, and misrouted packages.

This intelligent system ensures you're always ahead of potential issues, giving you the power to address them before they escalate, all from one centralized platform.
